## Title
Designer: Status Bar with Live Hints

## Summary
Add a status bar to show selection position/size, active Snap/Grid/Zoom, and short contextual hints.

## Scope
- Status bar line at bottom with x/y/w/h of primary selection.
- Indicators: Snap ON/OFF, Grid ON/OFF, Zoom %.
- Transient hint messages on actions (e.g., “Shift to axis-lock”).

## Acceptance Criteria
- Bar updates on selection/zoom/toggle changes.
- No modal pop-ups; unobtrusive UI; no export impact.
